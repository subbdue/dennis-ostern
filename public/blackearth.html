BLACK EARTH

Between 2003 and 2006, during his undergraduate days at RVCE Bangalore, Dennis sang, performed and composed music with Black Earth.

We miss him very much, but he continues to live with us through his music. Here are a few verses from Utopia - we imagine this is what he would have to say to his loved ones ...

Inspire a smile
See through this life
Visions arise
Of hope in the skies
And I would never step away
With you on my mind
I’ll always be here
In these Utopia skies

You can listen to the music he created, pictures and videos from live shows, and what the newspapers had to say about all this.
